# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project welcomes rigorous disagreement, independent replication, failed searches, corrected claims, and constructive criticism.

Participants are expected to:

- treat contributors with respect,
- criticize arguments and evidence rather than people,
- state uncertainty and computational limitations clearly,
- credit prior work and independent discoveries,
- correct errors openly,
- avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and personal attacks.

## Research integrity

Mathematical excitement is not a substitute for verification. Contributors must not knowingly mislabel heuristic, numerical, sampled, or finite-field evidence as a characteristic-zero proof.

Plausible counterexamples should be shared with enough exact data for independent verification. Negative results should specify the search space and avoid implying more coverage than was actually tested.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions that violate these standards and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and its contributors.

Concerns may be raised privately with the repository owner through the contact methods listed on the Neil Fox Agency GitHub profile.
